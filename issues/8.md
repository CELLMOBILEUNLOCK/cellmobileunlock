# Phase 3: Design Phase

## Objective
The objective of the Design Phase is to develop a clear and detailed design for the software application, ensuring it meets all requirements and specifications outlined in the previous phase.

## Tasks
- Create wireframes for the user interface.
- Design the architecture of the application.
- Review and finalize design documents.
- Obtain approval from stakeholders.

## Deliverables
- Wireframes and design specifications document.
- Architecture diagram.
- Approved design documentation.

## Duration
Estimated duration: 4 weeks.

## Dependencies
- Completion of Phase 2: Development Phase.
- Feedback from stakeholders on initial designs.

## Owner
Design Team Lead: [Name of the owner]

## Success Criteria
- Design documents are approved by stakeholders.
- Final wireframes meet user experience standards.
- Design aligns with technical and functional requirements.

# Phase 2: Initial Assessment & Analysis

## Objective
To conduct a comprehensive initial assessment and analysis of the project requirements and constraints, ensuring that all stakeholders' expectations are understood and documented.

## Tasks
1. Identify key stakeholders.
2. Conduct interviews to gather requirements.
3. Analyze existing documentation.
4. Prepare a requirements specification document.

## Deliverables
- Requirements Specification Document
- Stakeholder Analysis Report

## Duration
Estimated Duration: 3 weeks

## Dependencies
- Completion of Phase 1
- Availability of stakeholders for interviews

## Owner
Project Manager

## Success Criteria
- Documented and approved requirements by all key stakeholders.
- Clear understanding of project scope and limitations.
